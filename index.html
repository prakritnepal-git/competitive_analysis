The Battle for IT Orchestration

The landscape of IT Value Added Resellers (VARs) has shifted. It is no longer just about shipping hardware logistics; it is about solutions integration. We analyzed the "Big Three" competitors—CDW, Insight, and Connection—to understand their evolving value propositions.

CDW

The Scale Leader

Dominates through sheer volume, logistics excellence, and a "full-stack" portfolio. Recent acquisitions (Sirius) have bolstered their managed services, but they remain the go-to for transactional efficiency and hardware breadth.

Insight

The Solutions Integrator

Positions itself closest to a consultancy. Heavy focus on Cloud, Data, AI, and App Modernization. They win when the conversation is about transformation rather than just procurement.

Connection

The Relationship Specialist

Smaller footprint but higher touch. Strong vertical expertise in Healthcare and Government. They compete on agility and customer service scores, often acting as an extension of SMB/Mid-market IT teams.

Financial Scale & Reach

Size matters in the reseller channel. Higher revenue often correlates with purchasing power, inventory depth, and the ability to finance large deals. CDW is the clear giant in the room, with revenue significantly outpacing its peers. However, Insight shows a higher percentage of revenue derived from services, indicating a more mature "consultancy" mix.

CDW: The Powerhouse

  • ✅ Inventory: Unmatched warehousing and logistics. "Full Stack" capability.
  • ✅ Acquisitions: Aggressive M&A (e.g., Sirius) has closed gaps in security and services.
  • ❌ Focus: Can feel transactional for smaller clients; massive account rep turnover.

Insight: The Innovator

  • ✅ Technical Depth: Strongest in Azure, DevOps, and Data/AI projects.
  • ✅ Modernization: Helps clients refactor apps, not just buy servers.
  • ❌ Brand: Sometimes overlooked for basic hardware fulfillment compared to CDW.

Connection: The Specialist

  • ✅ Healthcare: Market leader in health IT solutions and specific regulatory needs.
  • ✅ Service: High NPS scores; reps tend to stay longer on accounts.
  • ❌ Scale: Limited global footprint compared to the other two giants.
